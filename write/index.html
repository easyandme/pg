Want to write for Plot Guru?

DEADLINE TO APPLY: DECEMBER 15

Every question inside Plot Guru is manually written. At this moment, we have 19 shows on the app –– but only 1 season per show. We need more content. A lot more content!

That’s where you come in. We’re looking for super-fans who want to share their excitement & TV-show knowledge with thousands of people across the world.

Here’s what you’ll be doing:

  • Watching Netflix (you pick your own shows!)
  • Writing interesting questions & trivia based on what you’re watching

Here’s what you need:

  • Access to Netflix
  • Obsession with Netflix
  • Access to Google Docs
  • Solid writing skills.

Here’s the time commitment:

  • Duration: December 19 – January 19 (1 month)
  • Approximately 8 hours of work per week (32 hours in total)

Sound good? Keep in mind we are a pre-funded startup, so this is volunteer work only. However, each writer will be credited in-app (your name will be displayed below each show you wrote) and on our website. And of course, you’ll be getting involved on the ground floor with an app that’s on the edge of something huge. We’ve been well-reviewed on Business Insider, Popular Science, and dozens of other tech sites –– we’re growing fast, so now is the time to get involved.

Here’s how to apply!

It’s simple. All you need to do is watch one episode of The Office, Bob’s Burgers, or How I Met Your Mother (your choice), and write questions while you watch. It’s a trial-run that we’ll use to determine how well you can create interesting content.

  1. Download this excel sheet.
  2. Choose an episode to watch. Pick from one of three choices:
    • The Office (Season 1 / Episode 1)
    • Bob’s Burgers (Season 1 / Episode 1)
    • How I Met Your Mother (Season 1 / Episode 1)
  3. Watch the episode and fill out the excel sheet with questions.
  4. When the excel sheet is completely filled out, email it to ethan@plot.guru

That’s all there is to it! We’ll be accepting applications until December 15, so get these in fast. On the 16th we’ll let you know whether or not you made it. You probably will though –– we’ll be accepting quite a large team of applicants.

Anyhow, we’re looking forward to working with you! If you have any questions at all, please let us know at ethan@plot.guru

Stay crunchy. And get that application in!
